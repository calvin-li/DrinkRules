MATERIALS

1. 26 cups (2 triangles of 6 cups on each side, 2 wash cups)
2. 2 balls
3. A F'N lot of beer
4. Table: approximately. 3'x6'

Rules of engagement (shots are thrown by hand, can be either bounce or air)

Rules


1. First throw is tossed by challenging/losing team ( team B)

A. If ball goes in, other team (team A) has chance to match

B. If team A matches, both teams drink cup fast as possible and attempt to make shot before other team. NOW its open table

If team A makes cup, team B must give ball back as fast as possible, while drinking the made cup as fast as possible

A. Team A has option of throwing ball before team B is finished drinking ,or,

B. Wait for team B to finish drinking. "While Team B is drinking": ( 2 scenario's)
-Team A makes shot- get both balls back -Team A misses shot- get one ball back
"throughout duration of game"

A. missed shots are open balls: meaning- PLAYERS ARE ENCOURAGED

TO WRESTLE/FIGHT FOR BALL until ball is in possession of either team

Once either teams cups are reduced to a total amount of 6 cups, cups are then put together in one pyramid

Knocked over cups are count as made cups, but team does not get ball back

Last shot, once the final cup is made,. there is a redemption shot,: losing team is aloud to shoot ball until they miss, only one player can take this shot

A. if each team has only one cup left, game goes into match mode: meaning once a ball is made, the other team has to match it,. this goes on
until shot is missed

Losing team drinks winning teams beer
