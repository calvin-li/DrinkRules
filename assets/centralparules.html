1. Throw ball into any cup, opposing team player must drink that cup.

2. Bounce the ball into cup, the opposing team player must drink two cup, but the opposing team may swat it away if fast enough.

3. Bounce off the ceiling into a cup the opposing team players must drink four cups of beer.

4. If a team member throws and makes the last cup of opposing team. The opposing team has a redemption shot, where he throws until he misses
the shot.

5. If a team member throws and makes the last cup of opposing team. The opposing team has a redemption shot, where he throws until he misses
the shot.

6. If a team member throws and makes the last cup of opposing team. The opposing team has a redemption shot, where he throws until he misses
the shot.

7. If a team member throws and makes the last cup of opposing team. The opposing team has a redemption shot, where he throws until he misses
the shot.

8. If you miss an opponents cup you must have a penalty cup at your side, you must chug this beer. Everytime you miss!!! (This makes for an
interesting game)

9. The point of our rules, is to have good time drinking, without anyone getting hurt, but still get drunk, very drunk.
